About Yoga

Yoga is much more than just a physical workout — it is a holistic discipline that unites the body, mind, and soul. Originating over 5,000 years ago in ancient India, yoga brings harmony between human consciousness and the universe. The word “yoga” comes from the Sanskrit root "yuj", which means to join or unite — symbolizing the union of the individual self with the higher self.

Yoga is a journey inward — a practice that nurtures balance, clarity, and peace within. Rooted in ancient wisdom, yoga goes beyond postures, inviting practitioners to embrace mindfulness and self-awareness. The practice integrates breath, movement, and stillness to awaken inner strength and serenity. The Sanskrit word "asana" refers not just to physical poses, but to finding ease and stability both on and off the mat.
